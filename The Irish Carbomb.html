The Irish Carbomb

A drink with a rich and controversial history, the Irish Car Bomb is the perfect mix of soft and tough to take the edge off. It is akin to the boilermaker in its "drop and drink" form and uses an Irish Cream and whiskey shot to set off half glass of Guiness stout. When you're drinking carbombs, nothing else matters.

Ingredients

    -Bailey's Irish Cream
    -Jamison's Irish Whiskey
    -Shot Glass
    -Beer Glass
    -Guiness Stout

STEPS

    1.Pour Guiness into the beer glass.
    2.Pour a half shot of Bailey's Irish Cream.
    3.Pour a half shot of Jamison's Irish Whiskey.
    4.Carefully drop the shot of Bailey's and Jamison's into the Guiness.
    Rinse and Repeat approximately 5 times for best results.
