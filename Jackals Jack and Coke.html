Jackals Jack and Coke

An American classic, Jack and Coke symbolizes a pasttime of subtle alcoholism and American commercialism that defines this great nation. A simple mixture of Coca Cola and Jack Daniels, this drink will wake you up and fuck you up in one fell swoop.

Ingredients

    -2 ounces of Jack Daniels
    -A can of Coca Cola
    -A Tall Glass
    -A Spoon
    -A Measuring Glass

STEPS

    1.Pour 2 ounces of Jack Daniels into a measuring glass.
    2.Pour 3/4 of the can of Coke into the tall glass.
    3.Pour the Jack into the Coke.
    4.Use the spoon to stir.
